Discover

Find the thing you did not know to look for.

Move from a surprising hook to a complete, plain-English explanation. Open a first-use definition, reveal a natural question, or follow a related idea when you want to keep going.

Daily idea

Free means genuinely useful.

Learn Free gives you a fixed deck of three worthwhile ideas each local day. One includes Read more and Questions; the other two are satisfying complete briefs. Notifications can bring those same ideas to you once or three times daily.

Trust

Sources you can inspect.

All 631 shipped articles include structured citations to authoritative sources that readers can inspect. Learn preserves uncertainty instead of polishing it away.

Study

Curiosity, given an order.

Choose a subject, area and course. Lessons build in a coherent sequence, optional recall gives immediate explanation, and Continue returns to the exact unfinished lesson.

From Rome and the history of the universe to flight, memory, markets, biology, language and negotiation, each course is designed for an intelligent non-specialist—not a classroom exam.

Calm by design

No account. No noisy gamification. No reading profile sent to us.

Primary content is bundled for offline reading. Likes, saved ideas and progress stay local to Learn, subject to Apple-managed device backups and StoreKit services.

Learn Pro

An optional one-week trial, when Apple says you’re eligible.

If Apple reports that your account is eligible, you can choose to try Learn Pro free for one week, including unlimited Discover, every Course, Library reopening and related-idea rabbit holes. Apple shows the annual launch price before purchase; it renews unless cancelled.

Learn Free remains available without starting a trial: three daily ideas, their definitions and Sources, real Like and Save choices, and one complete starter Course.
