Discover

Find the thing you did not know to look for.

Move from a surprising hook to a complete, plain-English explanation. Open a first-use definition, reveal a natural question, or follow a related idea when you want to keep going.

Daily idea

Free means genuinely useful.

One Discover notification each day opens that day’s complete article, including its fuller explanation, definitions, questions, sources and sharing.

Trust

Sources you can inspect.

All 631 shipped articles have structured citations reviewed against authoritative sources. Learn preserves uncertainty instead of polishing it away.

Study

Curiosity, given an order.

Choose a subject, area and course. Lessons build in a coherent sequence, optional recall gives immediate explanation, and Continue returns to the exact unfinished lesson.

From Rome and the history of the universe to flight, memory, markets, biology, language and negotiation, each course is designed for an intelligent non-specialist—not a classroom exam.

Calm by design

No account. No noisy gamification. No reading profile sent to us.

Primary content is bundled for offline reading. Likes, saved ideas and progress stay local to Learn, subject to Apple-managed device backups and StoreKit services.
