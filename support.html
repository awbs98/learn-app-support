Learn for iPhone

Support

Most Learn settings, purchases and reading progress are handled on your iPhone. These answers cover the common cases.

What is included for free?

Learn Free provides a fixed deck of three Discover ideas each local day, even if notifications are off. The first is the Daily Deep Read, with Read more and inline Questions; the other two are complete briefs. You can open first-use definitions and Sources, Like, Save and Share all three, and complete the included starter Course. Your choices are recorded locally, although reopening Library items requires Learn Pro.

What does Learn Pro unlock?

An active trial or annual Learn Pro plan unlocks unlimited Discover decks, Read more on every idea, Related ideas and full-answer rabbit holes, all Study Courses, reopening items from Library, Study reminders and the content widget.

How do I manage or restore a subscription?

Open the Learn Pro screen and choose Manage subscription or Restore purchases. Apple controls billing, cancellation and introductory-offer eligibility through your Apple Account. Deleting and reinstalling Learn does not cancel a subscription or create another trial.

How do I change notifications?

Open Learn, choose You, then Notifications. Discover notifications are available in Learn Free and Learn Pro at once or three times daily. Once daily delivers the Daily Deep Read; three times daily delivers the day’s three saved ideas. If permission was previously denied, Learn provides a route to iOS Settings. The rolling local schedule is replenished when the app is opened.

How do I remove local learning data?

Open You and choose Reset learning data. This removes local reading choices, Library history, progress and reminders. It does not cancel a subscription.

Something is wrong or a factual claim needs attention

Please open a support request. Include your iOS version, the Learn version shown in Settings, the idea or Course title, and what happened. Do not include payment-card information, passwords or other sensitive personal data.
