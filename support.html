Learn for iPhone

Support

Most Learn settings, purchases and reading progress are handled on your iPhone. These answers cover the common cases.

What is included for free?

One Discover notification per local day opens that day’s complete article, including its fuller explanation, first-use definitions, inline questions, sources and sharing. More than one alert on the same day does not create extra free articles.

What does membership unlock?

An active trial or annual membership unlocks the full Discover feed, topic filters, Likes and Saved ideas, related-idea rabbit holes, Study courses, Library, Study reminders and the content widget.

How do I manage or restore a subscription?

Open Learn’s membership screen and choose Manage subscription or Restore purchases. Apple controls billing, cancellation and introductory-offer eligibility through your Apple Account. Deleting and reinstalling Learn does not cancel a subscription or create another trial.

How do I change notifications?

Open Learn, choose You, then Notifications. If permission was previously denied, Learn provides a route to iOS Settings. Discovery notifications use a rolling local schedule that is replenished when the app is opened.

How do I remove local learning data?

Open You and choose Reset learning data. This removes local reading choices, Library history, progress and reminders. It does not cancel a subscription.

Something is wrong or a factual claim needs attention

Please open a support request. Include your iOS version, the Learn version shown in Settings, the idea or Course title, and what happened. Do not include payment-card information, passwords or other sensitive personal data.
