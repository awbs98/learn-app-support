Privacy policy

Learn works without a Learn account, advertising, cross-app tracking, an analytics SDK or a Learn-operated backend.

What Learn stores locally

Learn stores your Discover choices, saved and liked ideas, “show me less” signals, recent and seen ideas, reading activity, course and lesson progress, reminder preferences, active days and a limited daily-notification grant ledger in the app’s private local storage. These records make the app remember your choices and progress. Apple-managed iCloud or computer backups may include local app data according to your Apple settings.

What the developer collects

Learn does not send your personal information, reading history or in-app choices to a Learn-operated server. It does not create a Learn account, use advertising identifiers, embed an advertising or analytics SDK, or track you across apps and websites. Apple may separately provide aggregated App Store analytics and opt-in diagnostics under Apple’s own settings and privacy terms.

Subscriptions and Apple services

Apple processes purchases, payment methods and Apple Account information. Learn receives Apple-signed StoreKit information needed to decide whether a trial or paid subscription is active. It does not receive your card details. A limited expiry-bounded access snapshot and signed-evidence checkpoint are stored locally in Learn’s App Group so the app and widget can enforce access consistently.

Notifications

Notification permission is optional. Discover ideas and Study reminders are scheduled locally through Apple’s notification system. Without membership, one valid Discover notification per local day opens that day’s complete idea; several alerts on the same day reopen the same idea. Study reminders and the content widget are member features. You can change Learn’s settings or disable notifications in iOS Settings.

External sources

Article citations may open external websites in your browser. Those sites operate independently and their own privacy policies apply.

Deleting local information

Use Reset learning data in Learn to remove interests, Library history, reminders, Study progress and the daily-idea ledger. The reset does not cancel an Apple subscription or erase Learn’s limited StoreKit access-control checkpoint. Deleting Learn asks iOS to remove its local containers, but does not cancel a subscription or reset Apple’s trial-eligibility record.

Contact and changes

For a privacy or support request, use the contact route on the Learn support page. If Learn’s data practices change, this policy and the App Store disclosure will be updated before the changed version is released.
